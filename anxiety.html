What is Anxiety?

According to the Anxiety and Depression Association of America, anxiety affects around 40 million Americans a year, yet it is still an unpopular and sensitive topic. Anxiety, is a feeling of nervousness, fear or concern and it can develop from stress. Stress can result from having to juggle many tasks at once, like preparing for a significant event or exam, or from the bodyâ€™s response to possible danger.

General Symptoms of Anxiety:

Physical Symptoms:

  • Stomachache
  • Muscle tension
  • Rapid breathing
  • Headache
  • Sweating
  • Fast heartbeat
  • Shaking
  • Dizziness
  • Change in appetite
  • Difficulty with sleeping
  • Fatigue

Mental/Emotional Symptoms:

  • Change of mood
  • Trouble concentrating
  • Panic attacks
  • Nervousness
  • Unreasonable anger
  • Restlessness
